Products & Stock

Genfix Kenya Limited has identified reliable manufacturers of high quality products that are suitable for a wide range of clientele. To enhance our performance we have a direct relationship with the manufacturer, such that we have the privilege of being able to forward our technological requests, compliments and complaints directly to the them.

During the warranty period, we send all faulty products if any to the factory for replacement with new ones. However, this is on very rare occasions since most of our products are durable, serviceable, easily up-gradable, user friendly and quite affordable. Electric Power Equipment is relatively expensive BUT in the absence of a power backup, electricity by itself is cheap only and until one is hit by a power blackout.

Knowing that we are operating in a period of economic difficulty, Genfix Kenya Limited endeavored to come up with a solution by doing a wide survey in prices. As a result, the products that were previously highly priced have been replaced with products of similar quality at two thirds the price. This is not in any way at the expense of quality.
